OUR PEOPLE

Founders

Srinivas Beesetty

Founder

Srinivas Beesetty
Srinivas, founder of Kahe Vidushak, is a Theatre Practitioner and has been performing as an Actor, Writer, and Director. A trainee from National School of Drama, he holds a Diploma for Playback and Proscenium Theatre. He has performed and received accolades nationally at theatre festivals and competitions. His directorial Panchhi and Do Kaudi Ka Khel has garnered over 28 national awards.

Ankita Jain

Co-founder

Ankita Jain
Ankita, co-founder of Kahe Vidushak, a CA and MBA by profession, is a dancer and theater artist at heart. Along with a keen passion for dance and theatre, she has been involved with an array of art forms. She has recently become an enthusiast Yogi and tries to follow the practice diligently in the quest to discover her body better. She is a keen contemporary dancer. Ankita has performed in various plays across Bangalore and has received a few national awards as well. Stage, as she says, is her happy place!
