Kharaashein

Synopsis

Kharaashein’ is a hard-hitting literary masterpiece about communal harmony that is as relevant now as it ever was. Based on the insanity of riots and how they affect the psyche of a common man, ‘Kharaashein’ (meaning ‘scars’) comprises verses and short stories written by Gulzar! On the occasion of 6 month celebration of Natya Aalochan, we bring to you a rehearsed reading of Gulzar's 'Kharaashein'. The reading will followed by a discussion with the audience.

Playwright: Gulzar, Complied by Salim Arif, Asst Direction: Ankita Jain, Directed by Srinivas.
